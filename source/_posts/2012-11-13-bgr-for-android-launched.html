--- layout: post title: BGR for Android launched tags: - Android - Apps - Mobile status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- I've been working with the guys at BGR for a couple of weeks now and have finally released the often requested official BGR app for Android.  They've written up a nice story about the launch. This app was an interesting development because several years ago I wrote a news reader app specifically tailored to BGR called Genius.  Genius was well received, with over 10,000 downloads, before I started talking with BGR about the sale of the app and future development.  For the official BGR app I did a complete rebranding to match their new look, updated much of the app to fit the new Android UI Guidelines, and improved the performance of the article loading. BGR was a fun team to work with.  They had a look they wanted based on their iOS app, but were very open to suggestions regarding the UI allowing me to create a true Android experience that users will love. Download the app from Google Play.
