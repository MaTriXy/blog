--- layout: post title: Nexus 4 on Order tags: - Android - Mobile - Nexus - Nexus 4 status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '1' --- Google's latest flagship Nexus phone, the Nexus 4, went on sale this morning.  After reading reports of the UK version selling out in less than an hour, and going through the Google I/O purchase process before, I kept refreshing that page.  Some times it would load, sometimes it wouldn't.  Sometimes it would say "Add to Cart", sometimes it would say "Coming Soon".  After 15 minutes of teasing I was finally able to actually make it through the Google Wallet process and get one. If you didn't get one yet, keep trying.  I thought I lost it quite a few times before my order finally went through.
